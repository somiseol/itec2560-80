About Us

Welcome to Minneapolis Yoga Studio, your sanctuary for movement, breath, and community. Nestled in the heart of the Twin Cities, we offer a peaceful space for all levels to explore the transformative power of yoga.

Our Philosophy

We believe yoga is for everyone. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening a lifelong practice, our studio is a place to grow, heal, and connect.

Meet Our Teachers

Our certified instructors bring diverse backgrounds in Vinyasa, Hatha, Yin, and Restorative yoga. Each teacher is passionate about creating inclusive, supportive classes that honor your unique journey.

Community & Events

Beyond classes, we host workshops, seasonal retreats, and community gatherings to foster connection and well-being. Join us for a sunrise flow or a candlelit meditation and feel the difference.
